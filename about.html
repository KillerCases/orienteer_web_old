Orienteering is a sport popular in Skandinavia and Europe. Punch cards are placed at checkpoints located in forest areas or similar. A detailed map is provided to help participants navigate to each checkpoint. The sport has now developed into all kinds of variants including cycle orienteering and urban orienteering.

Ours is a new take on the sport, designed to make orienteering more accessible and flexible. Instead of only being able to compete at certain times and places, you can take your smartphone and tackle a course at anytime. For other folks it's just an excuse to discover a new area and get some exercise at the same time,

We're really excited that you're reading this! The plan is to post new courses every couple of weeks if interest is sufficient. It'd be great to get your ideas and feedback on new course locations, technical issues and other thoughts.
